**Title:** Astro 6 Release Highlights

**Key Points:**
- New Fonts API for managing custom fonts.
- Improved Cloudflare support with unified development experience.
- Live Content Collections for instant content updates.
- Enhanced Content Security Policy for better security.
- Upgraded dependencies and performance improvements.
- Experimental Rust compiler for faster builds.
- Experimental route caching for better caching strategies.
- Community-driven contributions and open feedback channels.

**Why it matters:**
Astro 6 brings significant performance, security, and flexibility gains, addressing limitations of previous versions and enabling modern web development workflows across diverse runtimes.
