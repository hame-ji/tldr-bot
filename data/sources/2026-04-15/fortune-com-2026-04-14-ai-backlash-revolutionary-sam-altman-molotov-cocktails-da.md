Title: AI Backlash and Gen Z Reactions

TL;DR: AI resistance is growing among Gen Z, fueled by job fears and personal stories of manipulation.

Key points:
- Gen Z shows strong apprehension and anger toward AI, especially after violent attacks.
- Job market anxieties and underemployment are driving negative sentiment.
- Data center protests highlight community concerns over local impacts.

Why it matters:
- The backlash reflects a gap between AI promises and real-world consequences.
- It underscores the need for transparency and responsible AI deployment.
