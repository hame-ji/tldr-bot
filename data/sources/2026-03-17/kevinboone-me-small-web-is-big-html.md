**Title:** Small Web Size and Its Implications

**Key Points:**
1. The "small web" refers to non-commercial, personal use of web browsers and servers.
2. Initiatives exist to reclaim parts of the Internet for private, ad-free use.
3. The Gemini protocol is a lightweight alternative, limiting commercial use.
4. Gemini uses specialized protocols and has a small, active community.
5. Aggregating updates from many small web sites is impractical due to volume.

**Why it matters:**  
Understanding the scale and nature of the small web helps engineers design tools that respect privacy and avoid overwhelming users with data. It also highlights the importance of community-driven protocols and the challenges of managing decentralized, low-traffic networks.
