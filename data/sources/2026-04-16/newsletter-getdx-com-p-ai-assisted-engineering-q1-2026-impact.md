Title: AI Transforming Engineering Productivity in Q1 2026  

TL;DR: AI is driving significant productivity gains and structural shifts in engineering teams.  

Key points:
- Engineering managers are shipping more code due to AI adoption.  
- Junior engineers save more time with AI than Staff+ engineers.  
- Quality and security concerns are rising alongside efficiency improvements.  

Why it matters:
- AI is reshaping roles and expectations for developers.  
- Companies are balancing speed with quality as AI adoption grows.  
- Monitoring shadow AI and guardrails is becoming essential.  

Evidence:
- Engineering managers doubled code output; junior engineers saved 4.9 hours/week.  
- AI adoption rates are now at 93% across industries.  
- Shadow AI risks are prompting stronger policy enforcement.  

Caveat:
- Results vary widely; context matters for each organization’s journey.
